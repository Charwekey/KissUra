    Why KissUra Started With Lips

    It began with a girl, a cracked lip balm, and a dream — to create beauty products that not only shine but heal.

    The Meaning Behind 'KissUra'

    "Kissura" is a name born from the fusion of two powerful words: Kiss – symbolizing beauty, boldness, and expression. A kiss is often the final touch to a confident look — the signature move. Aura – the energy you carry; your presence, your glow, your identity.
